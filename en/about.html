About

Hello, I’m Anastasia, 26. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture & Design. With a solid art background, I create visual stories using AI and familiar graphic tools — blending aesthetics, technology, and atmosphere.

My path started with classical design and digital art. Thanks to NFTs I found my voice — and the world responded. I’ve exhibited in Moscow, Dubai, New York, Japan, Spain, and Indonesia.

Today I focus on new AI-creative formats: fashion visuals, 3D avatars, music clips, and brand projects with bold storytelling.

I want every piece to be more than an image or a video — something that moves you and stays with you. I love experimenting, care about details, and believe technology should amplify creativity, not replace it. That’s exactly what I do in my projects.
